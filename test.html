Join the University Student Union (USU) Beach Balance for A Week to Unwind, Oct. 25 through Oct. 28, from 11 a.m. to 5 p.m., in USU 313. Book a Relaxonchair using the SRWC GO app, receive a welcome gift, and be entered into an opportunity drawing to win a massage!

Visit the USU Beach Balance daily for free spa water, drop-in wellness activities, and to find the BB origami crane. The first person to find the BB origami crane each day will receive a $10 Robek's gift card.

  • Monday: Origami
  • Tuesday: Art Therapy
  • Wednesday: Pony Bead Bracelets
  • Thursday: Gratitude Wall
