Description

Learn more about the LBSU's COVID-19 safety measures and repopulation plans. Curious about what the spring 2022 semester will look like? Let us answer your COVID-19 questions as they pertain to Long Beach State University (LBSU). Join Associated Students, Inc. (ASI), Student Health Services (SHS) and the Dean of Students for a Virtual COVID-19 Student Q&A on Zoom.

The Q&A will include a presentation from SHS Health Ambassadors, followed by a Q&A session with Dr. Kimberly Fodran, the SHS Medical Director and Dr. Piya Bose, the LBSU Dean of Students.

Come to hear campus COVID-19 updates, ask your questions and share your comments or concerns.
