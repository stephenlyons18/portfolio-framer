The Student Recreation & Wellness Center team is excited to be open and serving you. Our re-opening process occurred in phases, as we continued to receive approvals based on the State of California Covid-19 safety protocols and mandates.

For information on facility operations, programs and services, please view the website. If you have any questions, please email asi-srwc@csulb.edu.

[item title="Covid-19 Mandatory Safety Guidelines"]

All members of the SRWC must strictly comply with all applicable CSULB mandates, federal and state laws, directives, and orders regarding the COVID-19 pandemic. For current information on CSULB COVID-19 requirements, please visit www.csulb.edu/covid-19

All users of the SRWC must follow the safety guidelines below at all times:

  • All members must provide proof of full vaccination or weekly negative COVID-19 test
  • Prior to commuting to campus, complete the COVID-19 pre-screen questionnaire
  • Upon approval, show the green check mark to show this to the front desk staff
  • Properly wear a face covering that securely covers the mouth and nose at all times (unless in the pool). Gator masks are not allowed.
  • Wash your hands often and use sanitizer upon entry into the Aquatic Center
  • Clean and wipe down all equipment after use
  • Must comply with general SRWC rules
  • Log in to the SRWC Go app and click on the bar code icon.
  • Scan the bar code at the entry turn style.
*Failure to abide by CSULB and SRWC guidelines may result in suspension or termination of membership
