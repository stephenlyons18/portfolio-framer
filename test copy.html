The Student Recreation & Wellness Center team is excited to re-open and thank you for your continued patience.  Our re-opening process is occurring in phases, as we continue to receive approvals based on the State of California Covid-19 safety protocols and mandates.  If you have any questions, please email asi-srwc@csulb.edu.

Alumni and Affiliate Membership Updates:

As of August 2, the SRWC will invite alumni and affiliate members back to the facility. As a reminder all campus Covid regulations apply including the covid pre-screen. Alumni and affiliate members can access the covid-19 pre-screen for visitors here.

 

Covid-19 Mandatory Safety Guidelines

[accordion]

[item title="Rules"]

  • Strictly comply with all applicable CSULB mandates, federal and state laws, directives, and orders regarding the COVID-19 pandemic
  • Properly wear a face covering that securely covers the mouth and nose at all times unless in the pool. Gator masks are not allowed
  • Sneeze and cough into one’s elbow
  • To comply with all SRWC rules
  • Wash your hands often or use sanitizer upon entry into the pool facility
  • Members are allowed to bring in your own kickboards. Kickboards, goggles and large towels are on sale. Ask SRWC personnel for more information.
  • Prior to commuting to campus, please fill out the Covid-19 Prescreen Questionnaire. Upon approval, you will receive a green check mark, be prepared to show this to the front desk staff.
  • Log in to the SRWC Go app and click on the bar code icon.
  • Scan the bar code at the entry turn style.
*Failure to abide by CSULB and SRWC guidelines may result in suspension or termination of membership
